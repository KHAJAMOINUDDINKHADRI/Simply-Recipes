ButterMilk PanCakes

Indulge in a delightful breakfast experience with our mouthwatering Buttermilk Pancakes. Crafted to perfection, these pancakes are a harmonious blend of fluffy texture and delectable flavor. Our secret lies in the careful combination of high-quality ingredients, including all-purpose flour, baking powder, and a touch of sweetness. The result is a stack of golden-brown pancakes that boast a tender interior and a satisfyingly crisp edge. The magic of buttermilk lends a subtle tanginess that elevates the taste to new heights. Whether topped with velvety maple syrup, a medley of fresh fruits, or a dollop of whipped cream, our Buttermilk Pancakes are the ultimate canvas for your breakfast creativity. Treat yourself and your loved ones to a morning delight that will leave taste buds dancing and cravings fully satisfied.

Ready in

40 Mins.

Ingredients

11

Serves

3-5 People.

Tags : Breakfast Food

Directions

  • Step 1

    In a bowl, using an electric mixer, beat the eggs on medium speed until frothy.

  • Step 2

    Add the buttermilk, melted butter and vanilla and mix well.

  • Step 3

    Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt, and beat on medium speed just until blended. (You may have a few lumps, but don't worry about it).

  • Step 4

    You can also mix this together using a wooden spoon or whatever you have.

  • Step 5

    Heat lightly greased griddle at 350 degrees.

  • Step 6

    Pour about 1/3 cup of the batter onto griddle for each pancake.

  • Step 7

    Cook until the tops of the pancakes are covered with tiny bubbles and the batter is set, about 4-6 minutes. Flip pancakes and cook until the undersides are golden brown, about 4 minutes more.

  • Step 8

    Repeat with remaining batter.

Ingredients

  • 2 - Eggs

  • 2 - cups all-purpose flour

  • 2 - tablespoons sugar

  • 2 - teaspoons baking powder

  • 2 - teaspoon baking soda

  • 1 - teaspoon salt

  • 2 - cups buttermilk

  • 4 - tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

  • 1/2 - teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 - 2 tablespoon vegetable oil or 1 -2 tablespoon cooking spray

  • maple syrup, for serving
